Overview

Urban gardening is a simple way to grow your own plants and vegetables even in small spaces like balconies or rooftops. This guide will help beginners start their own green spaces in the city.

Plants to Grow

Herbs

Basil, mint, and parsley are great starter herbs that thrive in small pots and require minimal care.

Vegetables

Try growing cherry tomatoes, lettuce, and peppers which adapt well to container gardening.

Gardening Tips

  • Choose containers with good drainage to prevent root rot.
  • Use quality soil and fertilize regularly for healthy plant growth.
  • Ensure your plants get at least 4-6 hours of sunlight daily.
  • Water plants early in the morning or late afternoon to reduce evaporation.
